# Kenney — City Kit Roads (CC0)

72 models from https://kenney.nl/assets/city-kit-roads (CC0).

Road/bridge tiles are catalogued **reference-only**: CityBuilder roads are
generated procedurally from map data and locked (PRD §8), so tile models are
never auto-placed. The streetlights, highway signs and construction props are
pooled as normal street furniture.
